Porkchops

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Chicken breast
  • Garlic
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Whipping cream
  • Butter
  • Noodles of choice
  • Chili pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Cajun seasonig

How to cook

  1. Season chicken with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, cajun seasoning, and chili pepper flakes
  2. Boil water and add noodles
  3. Put oven at medium heat
  4. Add oil and butter
  5. Cook chicken
  6. Take out chicken
  7. Add garlic, onion, parsley, cajun seasoning, whipping cream, and paprika
  8. Add noodles to pot
  9. Cut and add chicken
  10. Eat food yay yippee!!!
