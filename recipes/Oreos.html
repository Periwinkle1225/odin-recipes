Deep fried oreos

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Oreos
  • Pancake batter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar

How to cook

  1. Add pancake batter, one egg, vanilla extract, and oil
  2. Coat oreos in mix
  3. Add enough oil into a pot to submerege oreos
  4. Cook till brown
  5. Put powdered sugar on oreos.
