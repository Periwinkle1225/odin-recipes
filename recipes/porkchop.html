Porkchops

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Porkchop
  • Garlic
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Chicken stock
  • Salt
  • Pepper

How to cook

  1. Add garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and flour to bowl
  2. Coat porkchops with seasoned flour
  3. Put oven at medium heat
  4. Add oil
  5. Cook porkchops
  6. Take out porkchops
  7. Add garlic, rosemary, and chicken stock
  8. Baste porkchops in the sauce
  9. Eat food yay yippee!!!
